Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about carport installation in Cape Town

Do I need council approval for a carport in Cape Town?

For structures under 40m¬≤, carports typically qualify as "minor building works" under SANS 10400. However, requirements vary by municipality (City of Cape Town, Drakenstein, Stellenbosch, etc.). We handle all necessary documentation and approvals for your peace of mind.

How long does carport installation take?

Most single carports (3m x 6m) are completed in 1-2 days. Double carports and custom cantilever designs typically take 2-3 days. Complex projects or challenging site conditions may require 3-5 days. We provide specific timelines during the quote process.

Are your carports wind-rated for Cape Town?

Absolutely! All our structures are engineered to withstand 120-150+ km/h wind loads, specifically designed for Cape Town's South-Easter winds. We use certified calculations and premium anchoring systems to ensure structural integrity in extreme weather.

What's the difference between a carport and shadeport?

Carports use solid roofing materials (Chromadek steel or polycarbonate) providing complete weather protection from rain, hail, and sun. Shadeports use HDPE netting offering excellent UV protection and ventilation at a lower cost, but don't provide rain protection.

Will a carport rust in Cape Town's coastal climate?

Our marine-grade galvanized steel and aluminum structures are specifically designed for coastal conditions. When properly installed with quality materials, our carports resist rust and corrosion for 25-35+ years. We never use standard mild steel in coastal areas.

Can I customize the design and colors?

Yes! We offer multiple roofing materials (Chromadek in various colors, clear/tinted polycarbonate), frame finishes (galvanized, powder-coated), and custom dimensions. We can match your property's architecture and color scheme perfectly.

What warranty do you provide?

We provide comprehensive workmanship warranties on all installations (typically 2-5 years). Materials come with manufacturer guarantees: galvanized steel (25+ years), Chromadek roofing (10-15 years), HDPE shade cloth (10-15 years), polycarbonate (10 years).

Do carports increase property value?

Yes! Quality carports typically increase property value by 3-7% and have an ROI period of 3-7 years. Estate agents confirm that covered parking is a highly sought-after feature in Cape Town's property market, especially in suburbs without built-in garages.
